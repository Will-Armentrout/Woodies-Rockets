	\begin{center}
		\textbf{Deriving the weight flow rate} \\
	\end{center}
	Specific Impulse Equation:  $I_{sp} = \frac{T}{\dot{m} g_0} $ 
	$\rightarrow$
	Weight Flow Version: $ \dot{W} = \dot{m} g_0= \frac{T}{I_{sp}}$ \\
	Solve: $ \dot{W} = \frac{900 N}{250 s} = 3.6 N/s $
